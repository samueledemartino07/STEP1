Step is a platform designed to take you, step by step, into the world of work.  At the top right you will find three main sections, each dedicated to a different aspect of your journey. The first is a job interview simulation: an intelligent chatbot will ask you questions, guide you in your choices, and test you as if you were actually in the company. A simple and engaging way to understand how to tackle real tasks, stress-free and with the freedom to make mistakes. The second section offers you a series of practical video lessons, perfect for learning new skills in a light-hearted way. Imagine mini-episodes, explained well and without difficult words, that teach you what you really need – from useful tricks to tools you can use right away. Finally, there is a page dedicated to one of the most important challenges: creating your resume. Here you'll find clear explanations, tips, and templates for building a CV that best represents you and captures the attention of those who read it.

How to make your Curriculum Vitae

       

In this section, you will be guided step by step in creating your first Curriculum.

       

1. Header and personal data

       
             
  • Name and surname
    •          
  • Tel. number
    •          
  • Professional Email (ex.: name.surname@…)
    •          
  • City
    •          
  • Linkedin/portfolio (if you have it)
    •        
       

2. Personale profile (short)

       

3-4 Rows to describe yourself and your background

       
             
  • Who you are
    •          
  • What are your ability
    •          
  • Future goals
    •        
       

3. Working experience (if you already have it)

       

The correct order is from the recently.           For all the experiences:        

       
             
  • Workpath
    •          
  • Name of the company
    •          
  • Duration of work
    •          
  • Short description divided in keypoints to explain your experience
    •        
         

4. Education

       

School and university from the recently.           It contains:        

       
             
  • Title (degree, master degree)
    •          
  • Istitute
    •          
  • Date of ending or expected date
    •          
  • Final mark
    •          
  • Main exam or course
    •
